Video ID: Unique identifier for the video.

Title: The title of the video.

Description: A description or summary of the video's content.

Tags: Keywords associated with the video to aid in search.

Category: The category to which the video belongs (e.g., Music, Gaming, Education

Thumbnail URL: URL of the thumbnail image associated with the video.

Video URL: Direct URL to view the video on YouTube.

Duration: Length of the video in ISO 8601 format (e.g., PT1H2M3S for 1 hour, 2 minutes, and 3 seconds).

Resolution: Video resolution (e.g., 1080p, 720p).
